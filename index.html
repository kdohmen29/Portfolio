Sound Board App


This app was built from the ground up using React. It uses an API call to get audio files from freesound.org and display them depending on what you searched for. You create a unique account and you can save as many sounds as you want into your own database.

Lunch Picker


A lot of people are indecisive as to where they want to eat. That inspired me to make an app to help! This app uses firebase for history storage and google places to give you restaurants in your area that have the food that you're looking for.

Train Scheduler

Marvel Trivia

Hang-man


Hang-man is an app that is pretty self explanitory. I made it so it will pick a random word from a word bank and display dashes until the right letter is guessed. Once the correct word is guessed, it will let you know that you won.

Crystal Counter
